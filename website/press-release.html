FINOS announces intent to form OSERA.

OSERA is the Open Source Enterprise Resiliency Alliance, a FINOS initiative helping regulated institutions bring secure open source into production at scale, with the evidence to prove it.

Announcement live 26 June 2026, 9:00 AM ET

Secure open source - in production, at scale. Operational resilience, without lock-in.

FINOS announced its intent to form OSERA with support from Deutsche Bank, Goldman Sachs, Moderne, Morgan Stanley, RBC, Sonatype, and TD Bank Group. Producing a fix is only half the job: in regulated environments, getting it tested, deployed, and proven in production is what delivers operational resilience.

Read the official FINOS announcement: Intent to form OSERA, the Open Source Enterprise Resiliency Alliance.
